## Security Policy

Passwords must:

- Contain at least 12 characters
- Include uppercase letters
- Include lowercase letters
- Include numbers
- Include special characters

Passwords expire every 180 days.

Multi-factor authentication is mandatory.

Company laptops automatically lock after 10 minutes of inactivity.

Employees must report phishing emails within one hour.

USB drives are prohibited unless approved by IT.

## VPN Policy

VPN is required when:

- Working remotely
- Using public Wi-Fi
- Accessing internal systems

VPN software: SecureConnect VPN

Supported operating systems:

- Windows
- macOS
- Linux
